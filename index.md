I am an assistant professor in the [Department of Economics](https://www.macalester.edu/economics) at [Macalester College](https://www.macalester.edu/).  

I am an applied microeconomist.  My research studies environmental policies in the agricultural and energy sectors.

I have held various affiliations currently and in the past, including with 
 the [Center for Agricultural and Rural Development at Iowa State University](https://www.card.iastate.edu/), 
 the [Dyson School of Applied Economics and Management at Cornell University](https://dyson.cornell.edu/), 
 and the [National Bureau of Economic Research](https://nber.org).
